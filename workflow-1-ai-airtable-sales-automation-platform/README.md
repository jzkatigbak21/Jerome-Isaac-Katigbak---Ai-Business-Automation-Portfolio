# AI-Powered Outbound Sales Automation Platform

An end-to-end outbound sales automation platform built with **n8n**, **Airtable**, **OpenAI**, **Jina AI Reader**, and **Gmail**.

The system automates company research, AI-assisted outreach generation, human approval, email delivery, follow-up generation, and outreach tracking through a modular workflow architecture.

---

## Features

- AI-powered company research
- Personalized outreach email generation
- Human-in-the-loop approval workflow
- Automated Gmail email delivery
- AI-generated follow-up emails
- Airtable CRM with relational database design
- Automation logging and audit trail
- Airtable Interface dashboards for monitoring and approvals

---

## Technology Stack

- n8n
- Airtable
- OpenAI
- Jina AI Reader
- Gmail API

---

## Workflow Architecture

1. Lead Intake
2. Company Research
3. AI Outreach Generation
4. Human Approval
5. Email Sender
6. Follow-up Generator
7. Follow-up Approval
8. Send Follow-up

---

## Airtable Data Model

- Leads
- Outreach
- Follow-ups
- Automation Logs

The Airtable Interface serves as the operational dashboard for reviewing drafts, monitoring pipeline progress, viewing analytics, and tracking sent emails.
