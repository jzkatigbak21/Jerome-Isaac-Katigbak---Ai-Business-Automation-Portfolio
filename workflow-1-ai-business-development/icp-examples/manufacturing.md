# Ideal Customer Profile

## Campaign Name
Manufacturing Process Automation

## Company Profile
Manufacturing companies seeking to modernize operations through workflow automation and AI-powered business processes.

## Target Industries
- Industrial Manufacturing
- Food Manufacturing
- Consumer Goods Manufacturing
- Electronics Manufacturing

## Target Decision Makers
- Operations Manager
- Plant Manager
- IT Director
- Director of Continuous Improvement

## Company Size
100–1000 employees

## Geographic Location
United States

## Business Challenges
- Manual inventory processes
- Spreadsheet-based reporting
- Poor system integration
- Repetitive back-office tasks
- Slow approval workflows

## Buying Signals
- Facility expansion
- ERP implementation
- Hiring operations leaders
- Investing in Industry 4.0 initiatives

## Objective
Find up to 4 qualified prospects that closely match this ICP.
