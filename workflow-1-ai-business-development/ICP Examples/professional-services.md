# Ideal Customer Profile

## Campaign Name
Professional Services Automation

## Company Profile
Professional services firms with 50–300 employees that rely on manual administrative processes and are looking to improve operational efficiency.

## Target Industries
- Accounting
- Legal
- Consulting

## Target Decision Makers
- Chief Operating Officer (COO)
- Operations Manager
- Managing Partner
- IT Director

## Company Size
50–300 employees

## Geographic Location
United States

## Business Challenges
- Manual client onboarding
- Repetitive administrative tasks
- Disconnected business systems
- Time-consuming document management
- Manual reporting

## Buying Signals
- Hiring operations staff
- Expanding to multiple offices
- Investing in digital transformation
- Looking to reduce operational costs

## Objective
Find up to 3 qualified prospects that closely match this ICP.
