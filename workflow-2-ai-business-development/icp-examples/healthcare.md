# Ideal Customer Profile

## Campaign Name
Healthcare Operations Automation

## Company Profile
Healthcare organizations with multiple locations seeking to automate administrative workflows and improve operational efficiency.

## Target Industries
- Medical Clinics
- Dental Groups
- Physical Therapy Clinics
- Specialty Healthcare Providers

## Target Decision Makers
- Operations Director
- Practice Administrator
- IT Director
- Chief Operating Officer

## Company Size
50–500 employees

## Geographic Location
United States

## Business Challenges
- Manual patient intake
- Appointment scheduling inefficiencies
- Manual reporting
- Repetitive administrative work
- Disconnected software systems

## Buying Signals
- Opening new clinics
- Expanding staff
- Implementing new technology
- Growing patient volume

## Objective
Find up to 3 qualified prospects that closely match this ICP.
